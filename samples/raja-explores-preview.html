Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before we dive deep, let's prove to you right now that your first trip is more doable than you think. In the next 30 minutes, you're going to pick a destination and see the actual budget breakdown. This isn't hypothetical—it's the exact math we'll use throughout this guide.

1
Pick One Destination (3 minutes)

Southeast Asia (Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines), Eastern Europe (Poland, Czech Republic, Hungary), or Central Asia (Georgia, Kyrgyzstan). Pick based on gut feeling—which one makes you excited? That feeling matters. Don't overthink it.

2
Check One Visa Requirement (5 minutes)

Open iVisa.com or the official government website for your chosen country. Search '[Country] visa requirements for Indians.' You're looking for one number: visa fee. Write it down. That's step one of your actual trip—not as scary as it seemed, right?

3
Build Your 30-Day Budget Skeleton (22 minutes)

Use this template: Flights (₹30,000-₹50,000 estimate from Google Flights) + Visa (₹5,000-₹15,000) + Accommodation (₹15,000-₹25,000 for budget hostels) + Food & Local Transport (₹10,000-₹15,000) + Buffer (₹5,000). Total it up. If it's under ₹1,50,000, congratulations—you're already looking at an executable trip. If it's higher, we'll optimize in the next sections.
