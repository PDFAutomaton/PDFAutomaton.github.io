Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before diving deep, let's get you a quick win. You're going to use AI to solve one specific problem you're facing right now—and you'll see results before you finish your coffee.

1
Identify your one biggest time-waster

What's something you do regularly that feels repetitive or drains your energy? Could be writing emails, organizing information, brainstorming ideas, or analyzing data. Pick just one thing.

2
Use a free AI tool to handle it

Head to ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini (all free to start) and describe your task. Ask it to handle your repetitive work or generate ideas. Be specific about what you need—the more detail, the better the result.

3
Refine and implement

Take what the AI gives you, tweak it to match your style, and use it. Spend 5 minutes improving it if needed, then actually deploy it in your real work today.

After this: You'll have completed one meaningful task faster than usual and realized that AI isn't magical—it's just a tool that works best when you know what to ask it to do.
