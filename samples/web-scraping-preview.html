Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before we build your full system, you'll validate the core principle: targeted extraction with minimal setup. This quick test proves the approach works for your specific use case and gives you a concrete foundation before tackling scale.

1
Define one specific data point you need

Not 'competitor pricing data'—'current product prices from competitor X's pricing page.' Be narrow. Narrow targets expose technical blockers early and prevent scope creep that kills momentum.

2
Choose your extraction method (no code first)

Start with a browser extension or simple HTTP request tool. Test whether the target site serves data dynamically (JavaScript-rendered) or statically (HTML). This 5-minute test determines your technical path before you write a single line of code.

3
Extract, parse, and validate

Pull the data, clean it into a readable format (spreadsheet or JSON), and verify it matches what you see on the live site. Document any blocks or unexpected behavior—these become your troubleshooting checklist for the full system.

After this: One confirmed data point in a clean format, proof that the target is extractable, and a written note of any technical friction encountered. This becomes your baseline for deciding whether your full system needs simple automation or sophisticated error-handling.
