Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before you commit to building a full pipeline, prove the concept works. This quick win walks you through extracting a single data set from a real source, showing you exactly what's possible without code or complexity.

1
Identify your data source

Choose one website, product listing page, or data table you need to monitor. Something you currently check manually or pay someone to track. Write down what information you actually need from it—price, availability, contact details, review scores, inventory levels—be specific.

2
Connect using a no-code scraper

Log into a no-code tool designed for extraction (covered in detail later). Point it at your source. Use the visual selector to highlight the data you want. No coding required—you're literally clicking on the information you need.

3
Extract and deliver to your tool

Run the extraction. Within 2-3 minutes, your data appears in a spreadsheet, database, or business application you already use. Set it to repeat daily, weekly, or hourly. Step away and let it run.

After this: One working data pipeline feeding you information you previously collected manually. Proof that automation is possible without developers, code, or technical friction. A clear baseline to measure time saved.
