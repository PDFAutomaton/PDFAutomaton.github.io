⚡ Quick Win — Included Free
Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before you read another section, do this first. Right now. Not after Asr, not tomorrow morning — now. This single exercise will give you a working lead list from Google Maps, a saved project file you can reuse, and the muscle memory of completing one clean scraping run from start to finish. It takes 30 minutes maximum. Make your intention clear: you are learning a skill that is obligatory for anyone who wants to operate with amanah and efficiency in their business. Then open your laptop and follow these three steps exactly.

1
Download and open Apify's Google Maps Scraper — free tier, no card needed

Go to apify.com, create a free account, and search for 'Google Maps Scraper' in the Store. Open it. In the Start URLs field, type a Google Maps search URL for a real niche and city you actually need — for example, 'restaurants in Lahore' or 'real estate agents in Dubai.' Do not pick a fake example. Use a niche from your current or next client project. This is your niyyah made practical — start with real intent, not practice data you will never use. Set the maximum results to 50. That is enough to prove the system works without burning your free credits.

2
Run the scraper and export your results as CSV while it runs

Click Start and watch the results populate in real time — this is the same moment you see in the channel's screen recordings and it still works exactly that way. While it runs, create a folder on your desktop right now called DATA_SYSTEM and inside it create two subfolders: RAW and CLEANED. When the scraper finishes, click Export, choose CSV, and save the file into your RAW folder with a filename that includes the niche, city, and today's date — for example, restaurants_lahore_2024-01-15.csv. This naming convention is the first brick of your system. Every project from today forward follows this same structure. The barakah is in the discipline of the small things done consistently.

3
Open the CSV in Google Sheets and delete every column except Name, Phone, Website, and Address

Upload your CSV to Google Sheets. You will see 30 or more columns — most of them noise. Delete everything except the four columns listed above. Then add a fifth column called STATUS and leave it blank. Save this cleaned file into your CLEANED subfolder with the same filename. That STATUS column is where you will later mark each lead as Contacted, Replied, or Converted. You have just completed one full cycle of the system: extract, structure, clean, and prepare for action. This is the workflow. You will repeat it for LinkedIn Jobs, for Craigslist, for any source the channel covers — the folder structure and the four core columns never change.

✅ Expected result: A clean Google Sheets file with 50 real, usable leads from a niche you actually work in, saved in a folder structure you will reuse for every project going forward. Total time: 25 to 30 minutes. This is not a demo. This is the first run of your data system — and it is already more organized than what most operators have after months of scraping.
