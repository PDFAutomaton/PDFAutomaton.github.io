You've done intermittent fasting before. Maybe it worked for three weeks. Maybe four. Then the hunger got louder, the meal planning felt like a second job, and suddenly you were back to your old rhythm—not because you lacked discipline, but because the system didn't fit your actual life.

Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before you dive into the 8-week plan, you're going to experience what sustainable fasting actually feels like. This isn't motivation—it's proof that your body responds quickly when you have the right structure. You'll set your fasting window, identify your first hunger trigger, and practice one simple fix. By tomorrow morning, you'll already feel different.

1
Choose your starting fasting window

Don't pick what you think you 'should' do. Look at your actual schedule this week. When do you naturally eat breakfast late or skip it? When is dinner early or flexible? Pick a 12–13 hour window that already exists in your life (example: finish eating by 8 PM, start eating at 9 AM). Write it down. This is your anchor.

2
Identify your hunger trigger and prep one fix

Hunger isn't one thing—it's specific moments. Is it 10 AM at your desk? Mid-afternoon? Right before dinner? Pick the time you think will be hardest. Then choose ONE: black coffee, herbal tea, a short walk, or a glass of water with lemon. Prep it now (buy the tea, set a water bottle on your desk). You're not fighting hunger; you're redirecting it with intention.

3
Set your intention and begin tomorrow

In Islam, we're taught that actions are judged by their intentions (niyyah). Take a moment and clarify yours: Why are you doing this? (Clearer thinking? Better energy? Fitting into clothes you love? All valid.) Write it down. This isn't motivation—it's your anchor when the routine gets hard. Begin your fasting window tomorrow and trust the system in this guide to carry you through.

After this: By day 2 or 3, you'll notice you're not as hungry as you expected. Your energy might dip slightly (normal), but your focus will sharpen. Most importantly, you'll have proof that this works when you have structure—which is exactly what the next 8 weeks will give you.
