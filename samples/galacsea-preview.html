Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before you learn the full system, here's how to experience salon-quality results immediately. This quick foundation will show you what's possible when prep and application work together.

1
Prepare the nail bed correctly

Wash hands thoroughly, push back cuticles gently, and lightly file the nail surface in one direction. This micro-texture is what press-on nails grip to—skip this and peeling is inevitable. It takes 3 minutes and changes everything.

2
Size and apply with intention

Select the exact size for each nail (should cover edge-to-edge with no overhang). Apply a small drop of nail glue to the press-on, hold at a 45-degree angle, then press down for 15 seconds. Rushing this step is where most people lose their designs by week two.

3
Seal the perimeter

Once set, run a drop of clear gel or top coat around the edges where the press-on meets your natural nail. This creates a water-tight seal that prevents lifting. This single step extends wear time from 7 days to 21+ days.

After this: Nails that feel secure within minutes, designs that stay intact for at least two weeks, and the confidence that you can repeat this exact process whenever you want—for yourself or for clients.
