1
Check and lock your device fingerprint baseline

Before you register on any earning platform, open your browser and go to coveryourtracks.eff.org — it is a free tool that shows exactly what information websites collect from your device when you visit. Take a screenshot of your results. This is your current fingerprint. What you are looking for is whether your browser is sending a unique, trackable signature. If the result says your browser has a unique fingerprint, do not panic — but do not register on any new platform yet either. This screenshot becomes your reference point. In Section 2 of this guide you will use it to configure your browser correctly before any platform sees your device. Doing this check now means you are starting with accurate information instead of guessing later why an account was flagged. Takes 5 minutes maximum.

2
Create your dedicated income email address

Open Gmail or ProtonMail — ProtonMail is recommended if you are in a region where Google services occasionally have access issues — and create one clean email address used only for earning platforms. The format that works well is something simple like your first name plus the word work plus a two digit number, for example ahmedwork07 at proton dot me. Do not use your student email, your personal social email, or any address already connected to failed platform registrations. Write this new email address and its password into a notes app or a small notebook you will keep specifically for your income stack. This single action — having a clean, dedicated email — prevents cross-platform contamination that gets legitimate accounts flagged as duplicates. Takes 8 minutes including verification.

3
Register on Remotasks and complete the orientation quiz

Go to remotasks.com and sign up using your new dedicated email address. Use your real name and real location — do not use a VPN during initial registration on this platform specifically, as Remotasks uses location verification during onboarding and a VPN at this stage triggers an immediate review flag. Once registered, navigate to the Radiant AI training tab and start the free orientation modules. Complete at least one full module and pass the short quiz at the end. This does two things: it activates your account from pending to active status, and it qualifies you for the higher-paying task categories that unverified accounts cannot access. Many students skip the training and then wonder why only low-paying or no tasks appear on their dashboard. The training is the unlock. Takes 15 minutes and you will see your first task feed before the timer is done.

The Barakah Blueprint: Why 99% of Students Fail Before They Even Start

Before you touch a single platform, you need to install the mental operating system that separates students earning $300/month consistently from those who quit after week two.
