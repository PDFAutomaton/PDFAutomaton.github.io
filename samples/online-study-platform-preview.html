Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before diving into the full system, here's what works immediately. This 30-minute protocol locks one concept into your long-term memory using the three-touch method that actually sticks.

1
Pick one concept you studied recently but feel shaky about

Choose something from your last 3-4 days of study. Something you 'understand' but aren't 100% confident explaining. Write its name down.

2
The Three-Touch Protocol: Explain (7 min) → Question (10 min) → Teach (10 min)

First touch: Explain the concept in your own words without looking at notes (7 min). Second touch: Find 2-3 past exam questions on this concept and solve them (10 min). Third touch: Teach this concept to an imaginary student or write it as a simple explanation (10 min).

3
Mark and schedule the review

Mark this concept in your study tracker. Schedule its next review for Day 3, then Day 7. That's it. You've now activated long-term retention pathways.

After this: You'll feel immediate confidence on that concept. When you solve similar questions days later, you won't have forgotten. This is what the full system does across all your subjects, every single day.
