Get Your First Result in 30 Minutes

Before we go deep, let's remove one major source of anxiety: uncertainty about your actual financial position. Right now, most people have a vague sense of 'I'm not ready' without actually knowing why. This exercise flips that. You'll walk away with your real numbers—not your fears, your actual numbers. This is also your first act of intentional decision-making (what we call niyyah in the framework ahead), and it matters. Start with Bismillah, be honest with yourself, and give this your full attention for 30 minutes.

1
Calculate Your True Monthly Burn Rate

Write down every single expense for last month: rent, food, subscriptions, insurance, debt payments, everything. Don't estimate—look at your actual bank and credit card statements. This number (your monthly burn rate) is the foundation of everything that follows. This is your amanah—your responsibility to yourself—to know this accurately.

2
Map Your Current Liquid Assets

Write down what you actually have accessible right now: savings account balance, emergency fund, investments you could liquidate in 30 days. Don't include retirement accounts yet. Just the money you could touch if you needed to. Seeing this number is powerful—it's rarely as scary as the vague worry in your head.

3
Calculate Your 'Runway Number'

Divide your liquid assets by your monthly burn rate. That's how many months you could live on what you have right now without any income. Write this down. This is your baseline. It's not your target, it's your starting point. The whole system ahead is about expanding this number intentionally while you build your freedom lifestyle.

After this: You'll have three concrete numbers in front of you: your monthly burn rate, your liquid assets, and your current runway in months. Most people are shocked by this exercise—not because the numbers are worse than they feared, but because they suddenly have clarity instead of vague dread. That clarity is where real decision-making begins. You're not deciding whether to leave your job today. You're deciding how to intentionally build to the point where leaving becomes the logical next step. Barakah (blessing and benefit) flows from clear intention and honest numbers. You now have both.
