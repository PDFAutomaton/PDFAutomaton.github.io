Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Monroe & Co. ("we," "us," or "our") builds custom client-onboarding guides for marketing and creative agencies. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

One placeholder remains: your business mailing address (in the Marketing and Contact sections). A P.O. box or virtual mailbox works — it does not need to be your home address.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information — your email address (and name, if provided) when you submit our intake form or contact us.
  • Business information you provide — your answers to our intake questions about your agency (your tools, team, process, and goals), which we use to build your guide.
  • Payment information — handled by our payment processor, Whop. We do not collect or store your card details.
  • Business contact information for outreach — publicly available business email addresses we use to introduce our service to agencies (see Section 5).
  • Basic usage data — limited information about how visitors interact with our pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To build and deliver your custom onboarding guide
  • To process your payment through Whop
  • To provide support and revisions to your guide
  • To communicate with you about your order
  • To contact agencies about our service, where permitted (see Section 5)

3. How We Collect and Store It

Intake answers are submitted through a form powered by Google Forms and stored in Google Sheets. Guides are delivered by email. Our pages are hosted on GitHub Pages. Each of these providers processes data on our behalf under their own terms.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Whop — to process your payment
  • Service providers — such as Google (forms, storage) and our email provider, who help us operate
  • Legal requirements — when required by law or to protect our rights

5. Marketing & Your Right to Opt Out

We may contact agencies at publicly available business email addresses to introduce our service. Every outreach message identifies us and includes a way to opt out. We keep your information for as long as needed to deliver your guide, provide support, and maintain our business records. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
