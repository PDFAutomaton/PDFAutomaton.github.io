Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

PDFAutomaton ("we," "us," or "our") operates the PDFAutomaton platform, which helps YouTube content creators monetize their audiences through custom PDF guides and affiliate partnerships. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Your name and email address when you submit our partnership inquiry form or contact us directly.
  • YouTube Channel Information: Publicly available information about your YouTube channel, including channel name, subscriber count, video descriptions, and publicly listed contact information, sourced through the YouTube Data API.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you interact with our platform, including pages visited and links clicked.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Match YouTube creators with relevant custom PDF guides tailored to their audience
  • Contact creators about affiliate partnership opportunities
  • Generate and deliver custom digital content products
  • Manage affiliate tracking and revenue sharing through our payment processor (Whop)
  • Communicate important updates about your partnership or products
  • Improve our platform and services

3. YouTube API Services

PDFAutomaton uses the YouTube Data API Services to access publicly available YouTube channel data. By using our platform, you also agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service.

We access the following YouTube data:

  • Public channel information (name, description, subscriber count)
  • Publicly listed contact information in channel descriptions
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions) for content analysis

We do not access private YouTube data, user watch history, or any data requiring user authentication. Google's privacy policy is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Whop: Our payment processor for managing affiliate sales and payouts
  • Service Providers: Third-party tools that help us operate our platform (email services, hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and maintain our business records. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Revoke access to your YouTube data at Google Security Settings

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
